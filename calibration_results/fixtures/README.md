# Tactile load adapter

The adapter transfers a calibration weight through a small central foot so the
weight base cannot bridge across the gripper's rigid borders.

- Default lower foot: 8 mm diameter x 4 mm high.
- Default upper platform: 36 mm diameter x 4 mm high.
- Place the lower foot at the tactile skin's active center.
- Tare the adapter by recording it without a calibration weight.
- Keep every part of the upper platform clear of the gripper frame.
- Use a rigid material such as PLA, PETG, acrylic, or aluminum.
- Verify the printed lower surface is flat and free of sharp edges before use.

Edit the four dimensions at the top of `tactile_load_adapter.scad` if the
measured skin or weight dimensions require a different size.
